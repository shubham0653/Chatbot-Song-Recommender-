## 🌌 What is Moodify?

Moodify isn’t just a chatbot — it’s your **emotional DJ**.

Instead of scrolling endlessly through playlists, you simply **talk**…
and Moodify understands your vibe, your mood, your moment —
then plays the perfect song 🎵

Whether you're:

* 💙 Feeling low at midnight
* 🌟 Celebrating a win
* 🔥 Frustrated after a long day
* 😌 Just chilling

Moodify listens… and responds with music that *feels right*.

## 🛠️ Built With

* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Backend:** Node.js + Express
* **AI Layer:** OpenAI (Emotion + Intent Detection)
* **Music Source:** YouTube Integration
* **Architecture:** REST-based modular system

---

## 🔄 How It Works (Behind the Scenes)

1. You send a message 💬
2. Moodify analyzes emotion using AI 🧠
3. Converts emotion → music intent 🎯
4. Fetches the perfect song 🎵
5. Plays instantly via embedded player ▶️

All in a few milliseconds ⚡
